Senior Lecturer and Researcher

Educational Technology | TEE, UDL, Inclusive Pedagogy | AI in Education

I am a Senior Lecturer with nearly 15 years of experience in higher education. My pedagogical philosophy centers on inclusive, student-centered, and technology-enhanced learning, with particular attention to supporting neurodivergent students. My core interests include Universal Design for Learning (UDL), educational technology, Technology-Enhanced Education (TEE), and inclusive pedagogy. Guided by Scholarship of Teaching and Learning (SoTL) principles, I lead initiatives focused on meaningful assessment, the use of AI in education, and the design of accessible, equitable, and engaging learning environments.
